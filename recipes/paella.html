Paella

Description

A vibrant and flavorful Spanish rice dish, rich with saffron, tender chicken, succulent seafood, and aromatic vegetables, all cooked together in a single pan to create a delightful crispy base (socarrat). Perfect for sharing!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large red capsicum (bell pepper), diced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes or 2 large ripe tomatoes, grated
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika (smoked paprika optional for depth)
  • A pinch of saffron threads (about 1/4 tsp), steeped in a little warm stock

Steps

  1. Sear Chicken: Heat olive oil in a paella pan or large, wide, shallow pan over medium-high heat. Brown chicken pieces well on all sides, then remove and set aside.
  2. Add Rice & Saffron: Stir in the rice, ensuring it's well coated with the sofrito. Pour in the hot stock and the steeped saffron. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Arrange & Cook: Gently stretch or roll dough to desired thickness.
  4. Add Seafood: Spread sauce, cheese, and your chosen toppings.
  5. Rest & Serve: Carefully transfer pizza to the hot stone/steel. Bake until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
